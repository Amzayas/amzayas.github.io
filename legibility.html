The Legibility of Type by Linda Reynolds

Digital Media
13.125" x 9.5"
Fall 2019

The Legibility of Type poster and booklet was a project designed under the instruction of Professor Daniel Warner at Kansas State University. The idea of the project was to design a poster and book cover using the same text, The Legibility of Type by Linda Reynolds. With the poster, I used a bold, sans-serif font and color to emphasize the title and an interesting arrangement of text to move the eye around the project. Throughout the booklet spreads, I used warm colors and blocks of color to create a professional aesthetic.
