Letter and Image Integration

Digital Media
13.125" x 9.5"
Fall 2019

The Letter and Image Intergration exercise was a project designed under the instruction of Professor Mervi Pakaste at Kansas State University. The idea of the project was to find text that mimicked a given image and to find a letter for that appeared cohesively within an image. Throughout the exercise, I constantly sought out letterforms that gave off the same aesthetic or emotion as the given image.
