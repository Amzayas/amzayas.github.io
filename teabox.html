Allie Zayas

Helina Teabox Design and Layout

Digital Media
13.125" x 9.5"
Fall 2019

The Helina Teabox Design was a project designed under the instruction of Professor Mervi Pakaste at Kansas State University. The goal of the project was to create and design packaging for a new flavor for a high end tea company, Helina. Throughout a series of critiques, I utilized imagery, textiles, and paper to create a box that was simple, sleek, and approachable for the Helina tea flavor, Late Night Lemongrass.
