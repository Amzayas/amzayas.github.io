Weather.amz.com

Digital Media
1920px x 1080px
Fall 2019

The website, weather.amz.com was a project in html designed under the instruction of Professor Anthony Warnick at Kansas State University. The idea of the project was to utiiize variable content such as weather conditions in an website using html and css. The process of designing this website involved creating wire frames and an initial design through Adobe XD and utilizing that information to create a website.
